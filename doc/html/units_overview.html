2.1 Frequency (hertz)

$$1 / s = 1.0 Hz$$

2.2 Angle (radian)

$$1 rad = 1.0 rad$$

$$1 rev = 6.28319 rad$$ $$2 \cdot {\pi} rad = 1.0 rev$$

$$1 ' = 2.90888E-4 rad$$ $$60 ' = 1.0 °$$

$$1 '' = 4.84814E-6 rad$$ $$60 '' = 1.0 '$$

$$kg \cdot m \cdot {s}^{-2} = 1.0 N$$

$$1 dyn = 1.0E-5 N$$

$$1 kgf = 9.80665 N$$

$$1 lbf = 4.44822 N$$

2.4 Pressure, stress (pascal)

$$kg \cdot {m}^{-1} \cdot {s}^{-2} = 1.0 Pa$$

$$N / {m}^{2} = 1.0 Pa$$

$$1 atm = 101325.0 Pa$$

$$1 bar = 100000.0 Pa$$

$$1 mmHg = 133.322 Pa$$

$$1 inHg = 3386.39 Pa$$

2.5 Energy, work, heat (joule)

$$kg \cdot {m}^{2} \cdot {s}^{-2} = 1.0 J$$

$$N \cdot m = 1.0 J$$ $$C \cdot V = 1.0 J$$ $$W \cdot s = 1.0 J$$

$$1 erg = 1.0E-7 J$$

$$1 eV = 1.60218E-19 J$$

2.6 Power (watt)

$$kg \cdot {m}^{2} \cdot {s}^{-3} = 1.0 W$$

$$J / s = 1.0 W$$ $$V \cdot A = 1.0 W$$

$$1 hp = 735.499 W$$

2.7 Electric charge (coulomb)

$$s \cdot A = 1.0 C$$

$$F \cdot V = 1.0 C$$

$$1 e = 1.60218E-19 C$$

$$1 Fd = 96485.3 C$$

$$1 Fr = 3.3356E-10 C$$

2.8 Voltage (volt)

$$kg \cdot {m}^{2} \cdot {s}^{-3} \cdot {A}^{-1} = 1.0 V$$

$$W / A = 1.0 V$$ $$J / C = 1.0 V$$

2.9 Electric capacitance (farad)

$${kg}^{-1} \cdot {m}^{-2} \cdot {s}^{4} \cdot {A}^{2} = 1.0 F$$

$$C / V = 1.0 F$$ $$s / {\Omega} = 1.0 F$$

2.10 Electric resistance (ohm)

$$kg \cdot {m}^{2} \cdot {s}^{-3} \cdot {A}^{-2} = 1.0 {\Omega}$$

$$1 / S = 1.0 {\Omega}$$ $$V / A = 1.0 {\Omega}$$

2.11 Electric conductance (siemens)

$${kg}^{-1} \cdot {m}^{-2} \cdot {s}^{3} \cdot {A}^{2} = 1.0 S$$

$$1 / {\Omega} = 1.0 S$$ $$A / V = 1.0 S$$

2.12 Magnetic flux (weber)

$$kg \cdot {m}^{2} \cdot {s}^{-2} \cdot {A}^{-1} = 1.0 Wb$$

$$J / A = 1.0 Wb$$ $$T \cdot {m}^{2} = 1.0 Wb$$

2.13 Magnetic field strength (tesla)

$$kg \cdot {s}^{-2} \cdot {A}^{-1} = 1.0 T$$

$$V \cdot s / {m}^{2} = 1.0 T$$ $$Wb / {m}^{2} = 1.0 T$$

$$N / \left( A \cdot m\right) = 1.0 T$$

2.14 Electrical inductance (henry)

$$kg \cdot {m}^{2} \cdot {s}^{-2} \cdot {A}^{-2} = 1.0 H$$

$$V \cdot s / A = 1.0 H$$ $${\Omega} \cdot s = 1.0 H$$

$$Wb / A = 1.0 H$$

$$cd \cdot sr = 1.0 lm$$

$$lm / {m}^{2} = 1.0 lx$$

2.17 Radioactivity (becquerel)
